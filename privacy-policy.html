1. Overview

Text Eraser for Photos (the “App”) is developed by Rohan Manocha . We respect your privacy and designed the App so that your photos and edits remain on your device. We do not operate servers for this App and do not receive your images or edits.

2. Scope

This Policy explains how the App accesses and handles information when you use it on iOS/iPadOS. It also covers limited information you may voluntarily provide when you contact us for support.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No photos or edits collected: We do not collect, store, or transmit your images, detected text, or edits.
  • No analytics or ads: The App does not include analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.
  • No account data: The App does not require you to create an account or share personal details to use core features.

4. On-Device Processing

All detection, erasing, and rendering occur locally using Apple frameworks (e.g., Vision, Core Image). Except when you explicitly use the iOS share sheet, your content does not leave your device.

5. Permissions We Request

  • Photos — To import images for editing and to save your results back to your library. You can change access in Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos.
  • Sharing — If you choose to share an edited image, iOS handles that action via the system share sheet.

6. Purchases & Subscriptions

The App offers optional subscriptions managed by Apple’s App Store. We do not receive your card or billing details. Available options:

  • Free tier: Edit up to 5 photos.
  • Weekly: $3.99
  • Monthly: $7.99
  • Yearly: $29.99

Apple may process limited information (e.g., purchase history, region) under its own terms. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 (or the minimum age required by local law).

8. Your Choices & Controls

  • Revoke Photos access in iOS Settings at any time.
  • Delete the App to remove locally stored data associated with it.
  • If you email us, you control what information you share; we use it only to respond to your inquiry.

9. Security

Because processing occurs on-device, your content remains under your control. We do not operate servers that store your photos or edits.

10. Retention

We do not retain your images. If you contact us by email, we keep your message only as long as necessary to address your request and to comply with legal obligations.

11. International Transfers

We do not transfer your photos internationally because we do not receive them. If you contact us and voluntarily share personal data, that correspondence may be processed in India.

12. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated “Effective date.”

13. Contact

Questions or requests: nyoargames@gmail.com

14. Key Definitions

“Personal data” means information that identifies or can reasonably be linked to an individual.
