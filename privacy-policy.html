Privacy Policy — Text Eraser for Photos

Last updated: October 6, 2025

Developer: Rohan Manocha

Contact: nyoargames@gmail.com

1. Introduction

Text Eraser for Photos (“the App”) processes photos locally on your device. Images are not uploaded to external servers.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No collection, storage, or transmission of your photos or personal content.
  • No analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking.

3. Permissions

The App requests access to your Photo Library to import and save edited images and uses the system share sheet when you choose to share.

4. On-Device Processing

All detection, erasing, and editing happen on your device using Apple frameworks.

5. Purchases & Subscriptions

Payments are processed by Apple. Plans: Free (5 photos), Weekly $3.99, Monthly $7.99, Yearly $29.99.

6. Children’s Privacy

No data is knowingly collected from children under 13.

7. Data Security

Your content stays on your device; we do not transmit it to servers we control.

8. Your Choices

  • Revoke Photos access in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos.
  • Delete the app to remove locally stored data.

9. Changes

Policy updates will appear on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
